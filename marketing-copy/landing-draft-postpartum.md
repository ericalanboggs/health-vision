## Solution Section

### A different approach

Summit doesn't ask you to be more disciplined. It assumes you already are — and gives you a system sized for the life you have right now.

1. **Tell us where you are.** Three months postpartum or three years. Five-minute setup, no 40-question intake.

2. **Pick 1–3 habits, sized to your week.** Not someone else's plan. Yours. AI suggests options based on your vision and your season; you pick what's doable.

3. **Daily texts as your touchpoint.** "Walk after lunch?" "Protein at breakfast — 30g?" "How'd today land?" Reply when you can. Skip when you can't. The thread never shames you.

4. **Weekly reflection by text.** Five minutes. What worked, what didn't, what to adjust. The plan flexes with the season.

5. **Coach Eric in the loop.** Plus and Premium tiers include monthly calls with a real coach — Mayo Clinic-certified, focused on your actual life.

**All via text. No app obsession. No "bounce back" language. Ever.**
