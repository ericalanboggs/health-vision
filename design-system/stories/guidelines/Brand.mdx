# Brand Principles

Summit is an **aspirational health platform** with a **"Nature-Tech"** aesthetic. We combine the clarity and precision of modern technology with the organic, grounding feel of nature.

---

## The Mission

> A sustainable, gentle path to the peak of personal health.

We believe in meeting people where they are and supporting their unique journey — not imposing rigid systems or creating guilt around progress.

### Positive Regard

We assume good intent, celebrate progress (no matter how small), and never create guilt or "streak pressure."

| Instead of... | We say... |
|---------------|-----------|
| "Your streak ended" | "Every day is a fresh start" |
| "0 of 5 completed" | "Ready to begin today's climb?" |
| "You're falling behind" | "Pick up where you left off" |
| "Days missed: 3" | "Your last check-in was Tuesday" |

---

### Wholeness

We believe people have the answers within themselves. Summit supports their journey — it doesn't prescribe solutions.

### Clarity

Clean interfaces, bold typography, and clear information hierarchy. Every element earns its place on the screen.

**Visual Clarity Checklist:**
- [ ] One primary action per view
- [ ] Clear visual hierarchy (headlines → body → meta)
- [ ] Generous whitespace
- [ ] Meaningful icons (not decoration)
- [ ] Accessible color contrast

---

## Brand Personality

### We are...

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| **Encouraging** | Celebrate every win, big or small |
| **Calm** | No urgency, no pressure, no guilt |
| **Modern** | Clean, tech-forward aesthetic |
| **Grounded** | Nature metaphors, organic feel |
| **Thoughtful** | Ask questions, listen deeply |

### We are not...

- Aggressive or pushy
- Clinical or cold
- Complicated or overwhelming
- Judgmental or shame-based
- Trendy or superficial
