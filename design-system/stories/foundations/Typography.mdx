Typography is a **design element** at Summit — bold, expressive headlines that feel aspirational and motivating. We use Inter for its excellent readability and modern character.

## Typography Principles

### Headlines as Design
Headlines should feel aspirational and motivating:
- ✅ "Welcome to Your Summit, Eric."
- ✅ "This Week's Climb"
- ✅ "Your North Star"
- ✅ "The Ascent Continues"

### Hierarchy
1. Create clear visual hierarchy with size and weight
2. Use Forest for headlines, secondary opacity for body
3. Reserve Moss for accent text and links

### Readability
- Maximum line length: 65–75 characters for body text
- Generous line height (1.5–1.6) for comfortable reading
- Sufficient contrast (Forest on white = 9.5:1)
