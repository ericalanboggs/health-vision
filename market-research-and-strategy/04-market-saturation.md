## Blue Ocean Features (Potential Differentiators)

Features where Summit can stand out:

- **SMS-first full experience** (only Sunnyside has this, single-behavior)
- **AI-personalized content recommendations** (unique to Summit's YouTube curation)
- **Vision Framework with exportable summary** (no competitor offers this)
- **Coach-reviewed AI content** (human-in-loop quality control is rare)
- **Flexible tracking (boolean OR metrics)** (most apps force one type)
