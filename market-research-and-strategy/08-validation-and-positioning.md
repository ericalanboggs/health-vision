## What Would Make Summit a Compelling Acquisition Target

### For Noom/WW/Calibrate (Weight Loss Players)
- Proven SMS engagement methodology they lack
- Tech stack for scaling coaching beyond app dependency
- Entry into stress/sleep/energy without brand dilution

### For Virgin Pulse/Wellable (Employer Wellness)
- SMS channel reaches desk-less workers (construction, healthcare, retail)
- AI coaching technology to reduce human coach costs
- Differentiated engagement in commoditized market

### For Practice Better/Healthie (Coach Platforms)
- Client engagement layer they lack
- AI personalization to add value for their 50,000+ coach users
- Potential to become "Shopify for health coaches" with SMS engagement

### Acquisition Triggers
- **$2M ARR** with strong retention (>40% 6-month retention)
- **Proven SMS engagement metrics** (>50% response rates)
- **Coach unit economics** demonstrating scalable human coaching model
- **Enterprise pilot** showing B2B potential

## What Would Worry Noom About Summit

1. **SMS-first engagement bypasses app fatigue** that's driving Noom's churn
2. **AI + human hybrid at lower price point** threatens Noom's coaching economics
3. **Multi-behavior without weight loss focus** captures users Noom's brand doesn't reach
4. **Vision Framework depth** creates more meaningful goal-setting than Noom's quiz
5. **Technical founder speed** means Summit can iterate faster than Noom's corporate structure
6. **Coach-as-founder** brings credibility Noom's Silicon Valley team lacks

## Bottom Line

Summit's existence doesn't threaten Noom today—but a Summit with **$5M ARR and proven SMS engagement metrics** would be a serious competitive concern in the "tired of apps" segment Noom is losing users to.
