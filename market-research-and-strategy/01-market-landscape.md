## Key Trends Shaping the Space

**AI integration is now table stakes.** 37% of 2024 digital health funding went to AI-focused startups, accelerating to 62% in 2025. Every major player has launched or announced AI features: Noom's "Avo" AI coach, Headspace's "Ebb" companion, Google's Gemini-powered Fitbit coach. Non-AI apps face commoditization pressure.

**GLP-1 medications are reshaping weight loss.** Noom Med, Found, Calibrate, and WW Clinic all offer GLP-1 access. This creates both threat (weight loss users migrating to medication pathways) and opportunity (Summit can focus on lifestyle behaviors where medication isn't applicable).

**Conversational interfaces are winning engagement.** Sunnyside's SMS-first approach, Woebot's chatbot (forms "therapeutic bonds faster than human therapists" per research), and Wysa's penguin AI all demonstrate that conversational formats drive stickier engagement than dashboard-style apps.

**Employer channel is mature but consolidating.** 76% of employees now have wellness program access. Virgin Pulse, Wellable, and Gympass dominate distribution. B2B2C remains the fastest path to scale for clinical validation plays.

## Dominant Players vs. Emerging Challengers

### Dominant (Established Moats)

- **Noom** ($3.7B valuation, 50M+ downloads): Brand recognition, psychology curriculum, but facing subscription fatigue and difficult cancellation reputation
