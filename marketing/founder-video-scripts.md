# Founder Video Scripts

*Voice: `ERIC_VOICE.md` (Eric to camera — hedge-then-land, no hype, no shame,
busyness not exhaustion). Two videos for two moments in the men-40+ funnel.*

| Video | Used in | Tone | CTA |
|-------|---------|------|-----|
| **Ad / Landing** | Meta founder ad (hook #3), `/lifestyle-changes` landing embed | Hook — a little higher energy | "Go grab the free guide" |
| **Drip** | day-10 nurture email ("Why I built this") | Warmer, lower-key check-in | None (day 13 makes the trial ask) |

**Why two:** the ad/landing video talks to a cold visitor who hasn't downloaded
anything — it ends by sending them to the guide. The drip viewer downloaded the
guide ~10 days ago, so "go grab the guide" is wrong; that video is a soft "why I
care" beat. Same person, different moment.

## 1. Ad / Landing video — "Why I built this"

*Direct to camera, ~75s. Phone-shot, no intro card, no music to start.*

**[0:00 — the scroll-stopper. Say it before anything else.]**
> "If your doctor told you to make some lifestyle changes — and that was pretty much the whole conversation — this is for you."

**[0:06 — who you are. Plain.]**
> "I'm Eric. Same thing happened to me."

**[0:10 — why the usual answer falls apart. No shame.]**
> "Every all-in plan I tried was gone by week three. Not because I'm lazy. Because there was no actual plan. Just do everything at once."

**[0:22 — the shift. The payoff — slow down here.]**
> "What works is smaller. Way smaller. One habit at a time — so small it's almost embarrassing. You run it till it sticks, then you add the next one. That's the whole thing."

**[0:38 — why you built it.]**
> "So I built the coach I went looking for and couldn't find. Direct. No shame. Still there on the days that get away from you — too slammed, slipped your mind, no time."

**[0:52 — the offer.]**
> "I put the first steps in a free guide. Made for men our age. Link's right here."

**[1:00 — the close. Small-step CTA, not a hype line.]**
> "Go grab it. Then go do the one small thing today."

**Delivery notes:**
- Carrying beats: **"Same thing happened to me"** (0:06) and **"so small it's almost embarrassing"** (0:22). Let both land with a beat of silence.
- Don't perform it. A half-smile on "almost embarrassing," a real pause on "not because I'm lazy."
- Dry aside on the "do everything at once" line is welcome; cut if forced.

**On-screen captions (sound-off autoplay):**
- 0:00 — `"Make some lifestyle changes." — every doctor, no instructions`
- 0:22 — `One habit at a time. So small it's almost embarrassing.`
- 0:52 — `Free guide → for men 40+`

## 2. Drip video — "Why I care"

*Direct to camera, ~60s. Audience already downloaded the guide, mid-nurture.
No hard CTA — day 13 makes the trial ask. Warmer and lower-key than the ad video.*

**[0:00 — open. Acknowledge where they are. No re-pitch.]**
> "You grabbed the guide a little while back. So you've already got the method — one small thing at a time. This isn't a pitch. I just wanted to tell you why I care whether it actually works for you."

**[0:12 — who.]**
> "I'm Eric. I built Summit."

**[0:16 — the honest reframe. The payoff beat — slow down.]**
> "Here's the thing. The guide works on its own. But knowing what to do was never the hard part. The follow-through is. The day you're slammed and it slips. The week that gets away from you."

**[0:32 — why Summit exists.]**
> "That's the whole reason I built Summit. Not an app barking orders. Someone in your corner on those exact days — a nudge, a quick adjust when life happens, a 'you didn't fail, just pick the next small thing.'"

**[0:48 — the personal close. No ask.]**
> "That's what I went looking for and couldn't find. So I made it. No ask today — I'm just glad you've got the guide, and I'm rooting for you. If you want someone in your corner for the part that's actually hard, that's what Summit's for."

**Delivery notes:**
- The hinge line: **"knowing what to do was never the hard part. The follow-through is."** Let it land — that's the guide-to-Summit reframe.
- Lower energy and warmer than the ad video. A guy checking in, not a hook. No CTA urgency.
- "the week that gets away from you" — busyness, not exhaustion.
