# Summit Weekly Reflection

**A pocket-sized coach you can install on the AI you already use.**

Most habit apps hand you a checklist and a streak counter. This is the opposite. It's a short weekly
ritual — the same one Summit's coaching is built on:

1. **Find your summit.** A one-time vision: what does better health actually look like for you a year
   from now, and *why* does it matter?
2. **Run weekly experiments.** Not goals. Not commitments. A few small things you're *trying* this
   week. The framing is the point — it removes the pressure that makes people quit.
3. **Reflect every Sunday.** What went well, what you skipped, one thing to change. Then set the next
   week.

It coaches in a real voice — direct, warm, a little dry — and it knows when to stop talking. It's
free, it runs entirely in your AI of choice, and your data never leaves your hands.

## A few ground rules it follows

- **Wellness, not medicine.** It coaches habits. It won't interpret symptoms, lab numbers, or
  medications — anything medical goes to your care team. (See `references/clinical-guardrail.md`.)
- **No account, no backend.** Your vision and habits live in a single "Summit Card" you keep
  wherever you like. Paste it back each week to pick up where you left off.
